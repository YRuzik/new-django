Black&White

Black Color

Classic black is the color of luxury and elegance. And he also embodies strength, stability, rigor and minimalism. As for the negative characteristics, the first association with black is death. She is related to depression, danger, coldness and anger.

Features of black color:
  • - emphasizes nobility and strength, so it is used by premium brands;
  • - it goes well with bright colors, can be both an accent and a background;
  • - it is ideal in combination with white and its shade – gray.

Black can be found during the sales of cosmetics and fashion clothing, sporting goods, cars and gadgets. Think of the logos of Chanel, Hugo Boss, Puma and Apple brands.

But when choosing a color, a lot depends on the context. So, WWF — the World Wildlife Fund — has nothing to do with luxury. With a black and white logo, it reminds of the importance of preserving and restoring the environment.

White Color

Like black, white is often used as a background. It embodies purity, freshness, simplicity and minimalism. But if applied incorrectly, it will cause feelings of isolation, indifference and emptiness.

What is attractive about the white color in advertising:
  • - it goes well with all other colors;
  • - creates a clean image — both innocent and fashionable;
  • - it helps to focus attention on details when it acts as the base color itself.

White is loved by eco-brands, is relevant in the fields of fashion and sports, is found in jewelry advertising. Zara, Adidas, Sony use it as one of the main ones.
